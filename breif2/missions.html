Curiosity

i

Curiosity is a robotic rover sent by NASA to explore the surface of Mars. It was launched in 2011 and landed on Mars in August 2012 inside the Gale Crater. The rover carries scientific instruments to study the planet's climate and geology, and search for signs that the planet could have supported microbial life.
