What Is Astronomy?

Astronomy is the scientific study of celestial objects like stars, planets, galaxies, and phenomena that occur outside of Earth's atmosphere. It uses physics, chemistry, and mathematics to investigate their origin, evolution, theoretical models, and advanced technologies to uncover the fundamental principles governing the cosmos.
